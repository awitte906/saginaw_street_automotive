OUR EXPERTISE

ENGINE REPAIR

You might need an engine repair when you notice signs like a check engine light turning on, unusual noises like knocking or grinding, loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, fluid leaks under the car, rough idling, stalling, declining gas mileage, or if your car feels noticeably different to drive, indicating potential internal engine issues.

TRANSMISSION REPAIR

You might need a transmission repair if you notice signs like: leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, strange noises when shifting, a burning smell, delayed gear shifts, trouble shifting gears, a check engine light, excessive engine revving, or if your car is refusing to switch gears properly; essentially, any indication of unusual behavior in your car's gear changes could signal a transmission issue.

DRIVETRAIN & AXLE REPAIR

You might need a drivetrain or axle repair if you notice unusual noises like clicking, clunking, or grinding sounds, feel excessive vibration or shaking while driving, especially during acceleration or turning, see grease leaks from the axle boots, experience a sudden loss of power to the wheels, or if your car pulls significantly to one side when driving; all of these could indicate a worn or damaged axle or other drivetrain components requiring repair.

SUSPENSION & STEERING REPAIR

You might need a suspension or steering repair if you experience difficulty steering, uneven tire wear, vehicle leaning when parked, steering wheel vibration, drifting, fluid leaks, bouncing or swaying while driving, unusual noises like knocking or clunking, a bumpy ride, or if your car pulls to one side when driving; all of these can indicate issues with your suspension or steering system.

BRAKE REPAIR

You might need a brake repair when you notice any of the following warning signs: squealing or grinding noises while braking, a soft or spongy brake pedal, vibrations when braking, pulling to one side while braking, a burning smell, a low brake fluid warning light on your dashboard, or if it takes significantly longer to stop than usual; these could indicate worn brake pads, warped rotors, or other issues with your braking system

ELECTRICAL REPAIR

You might need an automotive electrical repair when you experience issues like: dimming or flickering headlights, difficulty starting your car, clicking noises when turning the ignition, a dead battery, slow engine cranking, blown fuses, malfunctioning power windows or locks, a smell of burning wires, or if your dashboard lights aren't illuminating properly; essentially, any signs of irregular electrical behavior in your vehicle could indicate a need for repair

AUTOMOTIVE HVAC REPAIR

You might need automotive AC or heat repair when you notice your car blowing warm air instead of cold, hear strange noises when the AC is on, smell unusual odors coming from the vents, experience weak airflow, see visible fluid leaks under the car, or if the temperature inside your car fluctuates significantly, indicating potential issues with the compressor, refrigerant levels, or clogged drains.

DIESEL REPAIR

You might need a diesel repair if you notice signs like: black smoke from the exhaust, decreased fuel efficiency, increased engine noise, overheating, excessive oil or coolant usage, unusual knocking sounds, a check engine light, contaminated fuel, or problems starting the engine, which could indicate issues with fuel injectors, turbochargers, or other components within the diesel system.
