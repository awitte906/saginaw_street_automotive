OUR EXPERTISE

GASOLINE & DIESEL ENGINE REPAIR

You might need an engine repair when you notice signs like a check engine light turning on, unusual noises like knocking or grinding, loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, fluid leaks under the car, rough idling, stalling, declining gas mileage, or if your car feels noticeably different to drive, indicating potential internal engine issues.

TRANSMISSION REPAIR

You might need a transmission repair if you notice signs like: leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, strange noises when shifting, delayed gear shifts, excessive engine revving, or if your car is refusing to switch gears properly; essentially, any indication of unusual behavior in your car's gear changes could signal a transmission issue.

DRIVETRAIN & AXLE REPAIR

You might need a drivetrain or axle repair if you notice unusual noises like clicking, clunking, or grinding sounds, feel excessive vibration or shaking while driving, especially during acceleration or turning, see grease leaks from the axle boots or experience a sudden loss of power to the wheels; all of these could indicate drivetrain components requiring repair.

SUSPENSION & STEERING REPAIR

You might need a suspension or steering repair if you experience difficulty steering, uneven tire wear, vehicle leaning when parked, steering wheel vibration, drifting, fluid leaks, bouncing or swaying while driving; all of these can indicate issues with your suspension or steering system.

BRAKE REPAIR

You might need a brake repair when you notice any of the following warning signs: squealing or grinding noises while braking, a soft or spongy brake pedal, vibrations when braking, pulling to one side while braking, a burning smell, a low brake fluid warning light on your dashboard, or if it takes longer to stop than usual; these could indicate worn brake pads, warped rotors, or other issues with your braking system.

ELECTRICAL REPAIR

You might need an automotive electrical repair when you experience issues like: dimming or flickering headlights, difficulty starting your car, clicking noises when turning the ignition, a dead battery, slow engine cranking, blown fuses, malfunctioning power windows or locks, a smell of burning wires, or if your dashboard lights aren't illuminating properly; essentially, any signs of irregular electrical behavior in your vehicle could indicate a need for repair.

AUTOMOTIVE HVAC REPAIR

You might need automotive AC or heat repair when you notice your car blowing warm air instead of cold, hear strange noises when the AC is on, smell unusual odors coming from the vents, experience weak airflow, see visible fluid leaks under the car, or if the temperature inside your car fluctuates significantly, indicating potential issues with the compressor, refrigerant levels, or clogged drains.

TIRE SERVICES

We provide tire sales, mounting, balancing, alignments and more. You might need your tires looked at if you notice signs like a low pressure dash light, flat tires, uneven wear, low or no tread remaining, vibration when driving or physical deformaties in tires, such as bulges, flat spots, bald spots or exposed wires. We are here to diagnose tire issues and help remediate them.
