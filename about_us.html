Welcome to Saginaw Street Automotive, your local mechanic shop located in the heart of Norway, MI..

Owned and operated by Andy Adams, we pride ourselves on delivering quality, trustworthy auto repair services to the community and surrounding areas.

Though Saginaw Street Automotive was founded in 2000, the building carries a much richer history. Originally built in 1926, the building was the home of Asselin's Dairy. The photo above showcases the iconic 65-foot-tall milk bottle that once stood as a beacon for the dairy and the town. Though the milk bottle is no longer standing, the building has been carefully renovated and restored to serve a new purpose: keeping your vehicles running smoothly.

What sets us apart:

- Honest and Transparent Service: We keep you informed every step of the way, ensuring you understand the work needed and the costs involved.

- Experienced Technicians: Our certified team has the skills and knowledge to handle all makes and models.

- Community Focused: As a local business, we take pride in serving the local community and fostering long-term relationships with our customers.

Whether you need a simple oil change, brake service, engine diagnostics, or more extensive repairs, Saginaw Street Automotive is here to help. Trust us to keep your vehicle performing at its best!

Call or visit us today and experience the difference of working with a shop that truly cares.
